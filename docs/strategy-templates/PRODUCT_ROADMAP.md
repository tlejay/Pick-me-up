## Feature Prioritization Framework

### RICE Score Calculation
**RICE = (Reach × Impact × Confidence) ÷ Effort**

| Feature | Reach | Impact | Confidence | Effort | RICE Score | Priority |
|---------|-------|--------|------------|--------|------------|----------|
| Loyalty Program | 1000 | 3 | 80% | 8 | 300 | P0 |
| Order History | 800 | 2 | 100% | 4 | 400 | P0 |
| Inventory Mgmt | 50 | 3 | 90% | 6 | 22.5 | P1 |
| Social Login | 500 | 1 | 70% | 2 | 175 | P1 |

**Scoring Guide:**
- **Reach:** How many users/stores affected per quarter
- **Impact:** 3=Massive, 2=High, 1=Medium, 0.5=Low
- **Confidence:** % confidence in estimates (100%, 80%, 50%)
- **Effort:** Person-weeks required
